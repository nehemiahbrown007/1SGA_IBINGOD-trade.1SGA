Ammunition is more than a consumable; it is the fundamental unit of kinetic sovereignty. Below is the 1saa definitive guide to calibers, ballistics, and the industrial base that powers global security.

The Evolution of the Round

900 AD: The Fire Lance

The dawn of chemical propulsion. Chinese alchemists utilize bamboo tubes and black powder to launch pottery shards.

1400s: The Cast Lead Ball

Standardization begins. Molten lead is formed into spheres for smoothbore muskets, defining warfare for four centuries.

1841: The Minié Ball

The birth of precision. Conical bullets with hollow bases expand into rifling, tripling effective engagement ranges.

1882: The Full Metal Jacket

Eduard Rubin encases lead in copper alloy, allowing for higher velocities without barrel fouling. Modernity arrives.

2026: Smart Energetics

1GAA leads the shift toward precision-guided small-arms rounds and eco-friendly primer technologies.
